A LITTLE ABOUT ME

My relationship with technology is akin to having that childhood friend you’ve never looked at in “that way” until your adult life — wondering why this didn’t happen sooner. I’ve always been a builder: leading middle school lego robotics team competitions and constructing every computer I ever owned since high school. At my previous employments, I had always provided technological solutions that improved company productivity by transforming outdated systems.

My ethos has always been to elevate my community. At CUNY, I was selected for a team that led and beta tested a multi-million dollar project that would replace the admission legacy systems. My contributions to the new admissions system enhanced the user experience of millions of future student applicants — actualizing my decision to become a software developer.
